Sweet & Sour Sauce (For Egg Rolls)
Serving Size : 12   

  Amount Measure      Ingredient
-------- ------------ --------------------------------
  1 1/2          Cups         Apricot nectar
  1                Cup           Pineapple juice
                       
     1/2           Cup           Brown sugar
     1/2           Cup           Honey
     1/2     Teaspoon       Salt
  1              Pinch           Ground Ginger
  3        Tablespoons       Soy sauce
                       
  2        Tablespoons       Vinegar
     1/2           Cup           Granulated Sugar


[1) Combine the Apricot Nectar and Pineapple juice in a pot.

[2) Add Brown Sugar, Honey, Salt, Ginger and Soy.....Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until the brown sugar disolves.
Remove from heat...

[3) Add Vinegar and granulated sugar, stirring to combine.....cool and chill.
